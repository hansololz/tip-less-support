Tip Less

A simple tip calculator, made with the aim to tip less.

Calculate tips quickly and easily. Whether you want to use your camera to grab the total or manually enter amounts, Tip Less helps you figure out the right tip while keeping more money in your pocket.

Features

  • Find amount to tip using your camera - just point and capture the total from your receipt.
  • Calculate tip with both percentage and fixed amounts - choose what works best for you.
  • Calculate tip while excluding taxes and fees - tip on the actual service, not the extras.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Tip Less is designed with privacy as a core principle.

All your data stays on your device. Tip Less stores all data locally on your device and does not send any data to external servers.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, calculation history, or camera data to any external services. The camera is used solely to capture receipt totals on your device - images are processed locally and are never uploaded anywhere.

No analytics, no tracking, no data collection. Just a simple tip calculator that respects your privacy.
